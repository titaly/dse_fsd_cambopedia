About cambopedia

cambopedia is an online free content project helping create a world in which everyone can freely share in the sum of all knowledge. The name "cambopedia" is a blending of the words cambo (a word which refers to a name of a country called “Cambodia”). cambopedia's articles provide links designed to guide the user to related pages with additional information.

cambopedia is written collaboratively by largely anonymous volunteers. Anyone with Internet access can write and make changes to cambopedia articles, except in limited cases where editing is restricted to prevent further disruption or vandalism.
